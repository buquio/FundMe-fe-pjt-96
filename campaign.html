The Imapact Foundation

Project 1000 is a donation goal aimed at sponsoring 1000 kids through school in the rural community of Makoko. Parents in this neighbourhood struggle to place education as an importance under the weight of daily survival due to the low income earnings associated with the community. The Impact foundation has been created to support these parents and encourage the children.
